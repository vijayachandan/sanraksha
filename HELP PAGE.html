OUR FEATURES

  1. Virtual Health Assistant or Chatbot: A chatbot that provides health information, answers common questions, offers simple fitness routines, or suggests healthy recipes.
  2. Appointment Scheduling with Health Professionals: An appointment booking system that allows users to schedule virtual or in-person appointments with health professionals (e.g., doctors, dietitians, fitness trainers).
  3. Interactive Symptom Checker: A tool where users can input symptoms to get potential causes or recommendations on whether to consult a doctor.
  4. Health Calculators and Assessment Tools: Provide tools like BMI calculators, calorie counters, hydration trackers, and other health-related assessments.
  5. Integration with Wearable Devices: Sync data from popular fitness trackers (like Fitbit, Apple Watch, or Google Fit) to provide more comprehensive health tracking.
  6. Emergency Health Information: Include a section for emergency health information, like what to do in case of a heart attack, stroke, or other critical situations, along with emergency contact numbers.
  7. Personal Health Record Management: Allow users to securely upload and manage their personal health records and share them with healthcare providers.
  8. Local Resources Finder: A tool to find nearby gyms, parks, clinics, pharmacies, or healthy restaurants.
